pjsip.org News: Oct 8th, 2006

DNS SRV Support

Just added DNS SRV support in PJSIP. The PJSIP DNS SRV resolution is based on PJLIB-UTIL DNS Asynchronous/Caching Engine and among other things it supports the following:

  • Asynchronous
  • Caching
  • Load-balancing
  • Parallel queries
  • Query Aggregation
  • Multiple Parallel/Back-up Nameservers
  • etc.
